Cybersecurity Tip: Use Strong Passwords

May 29, 2025

Passwords like 123456 or password are shockingly still common—and easy to crack.

  • Use at least 12 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid using names, birthdates, or common words.
  • Use a password manager to generate and store complex passwords.

Bonus: Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) wherever possible for extra protection.
