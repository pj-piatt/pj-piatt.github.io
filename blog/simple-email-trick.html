A Simple Email Tip Every User Should Know

May 31, 2025

Have you ever hit "Reply All" and instantly regretted it? You're not alone. While it might seem harmless, replying to everyone can clutter inboxes and confuse your coworkers.

Try this instead:

  • Only reply to the sender unless multiple people genuinely need the update.
  • If your reply includes private or sensitive information, double-check the recipient list.

One small change in your habits can make communication smoother across your whole team!
