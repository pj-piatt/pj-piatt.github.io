🛠️ Quick IT Tips for End Users

Published: June 1, 2025

Restart Before You Panic

Always try restarting your computer—it solves many issues like slow performance, frozen apps, or connectivity problems.

Double Check the Obvious

Make sure your device is plugged in, cables are secure, and your Wi-Fi is connected. It's easy to miss the basics when you’re in a hurry!

Use Ctrl + F

Need to find something quickly in a document or webpage? Press Ctrl + F (or Command + F on Mac) and type your keyword.
