Always Lock Your Screen—Here’s Why

May 30, 2025

Even if you're away for just a minute, leaving your screen unlocked can put sensitive information at risk.

  • Press Windows + L (or Control + Command + Q on Mac) to lock instantly.
  • Get in the habit—lock every time you step away.

It takes two seconds and could save hours of cleanup if someone snoops or makes changes without your knowledge.
